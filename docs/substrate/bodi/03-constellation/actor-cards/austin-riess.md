# Actor Card: Austin Riess

**Domain:** Creator Economy
**Confidence:** 2/5 (Niche Specialist)

## Profile
Creator-economy strategist focused on creator-owned platforms and social-commerce. Advocates for creators building infrastructure they control rather than renting reach on third-party networks.

## Key Contributions
- **Creator-Owned Platforms:** Champions owned channels (sites, apps, direct lists) as the stable base layer beneath rented social reach.
- **Social-Commerce Strategy:** Connects content directly to commerce, shortening the path from audience to purchase.
- **Ownership Posture:** Frames platform dependency as a risk to be designed out of a creator's business.

## Relevance to BODI
Reinforces the case for Rob owning his audience relationship (site, email, product) rather than depending solely on the YouTube algorithm for distribution and revenue.
