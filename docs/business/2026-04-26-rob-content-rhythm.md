## 1. Executive Summary

This document defines Rob's production and publishing rhythm across the three-pillar architecture:

1. **Chess / Hokage:** adult-improver chess climb, tactical study, game review, and community play.
2. **Fitness / Legion:** training, nutrition, form, physical discipline, and coaching conversion.
3. **Bridge / Discipline:** proof that improvement principles transfer between chess and fitness.

The cadence is intentionally simple:

| Day | Pillar | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Chess | Start the week with the primary Hokage teaching or climb artifact. |
| Tuesday | Fitness | Publish a practical Legion artifact. |
| Wednesday | Chess | Community, analysis, short lesson, or live-training follow-up. |
| Thursday | Fitness | Publish the second Legion artifact. |
| Friday | Chess | Weekend-facing chess story, challenge, Boss Battle, or Loot Drop. |
| Saturday | Bridge | Cross-pillar synthesis lane; major BR-1 piece every two weeks. |
| Sunday | Quest Log | Owned-media recap by MP-7 track and next-week reset. |

The days are fixed so the audience knows what to expect. The volume inside each day flexes with MP-10 once Rob's real weekly hours are measured.

## 2. Operating Rules

### 2.1 Fixed slots, variable weight

The rhythm defines **which pillar owns each day**, not how large the artifact must be. A Monday chess slot can be a 12-minute YouTube lesson during a high-capacity week or a 60-second tactic breakdown during a constrained week.

### 2.2 Bridge does not replace the pillars

Bridge content only works if the audience has seen enough pure chess and pure fitness to understand what is being connected. Saturday is always the bridge lane, but the major BR-1 artifact publishes **every two weeks** until the weekday rhythm is stable.

Off-week Saturdays are still bridge-facing:

- outline the next BR-1 piece,
- clip a bridge moment from the week,
- ask the audience a bridge question,
- gather proof for the next Sunday Quest Log.

### 2.3 Sunday is owned-media synthesis

The Quest Log is not another social post. It is the Sunday owned-media recap ritual. MP-7 still governs delivery: chess subscribers can receive a Hokage-focused edition, fitness subscribers can receive a Legion-focused edition, and bridge-opted subscribers can receive the full cross-pillar synthesis.

- recap the relevant pillar work,
- name the bridge principle when the subscriber has opted into or engaged with bridge material,
- link the week's best artifacts for that track,
- preview the next seven days,
- carry one primary CTA.

### 2.4 Algorithm-sensitive channels stay protected

This cadence inherits the MP-1 routing decision:

| Surface | Routing |
|---|---|
| YouTube long-form | Separate chess and fitness channels/surfaces where possible. |
| Short-form video | Unified personality feed is acceptable. |
| Twitch / live | Unified training-arc channel is acceptable. |
| Newsletter | Shared database with segmented Quest Log editions. |
| Discord / Skool | Unified community with pillar-specific rooms. |

The calendar is cross-pillar; the algorithmic packaging still respects platform behavior.

## 3. Weekly Calendar

| Day | Pillar | Primary artifact | Secondary artifact | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Chess | Jutsu lesson, game analysis, Road to 1500 entry, or tactic breakdown | Short cutdown for Shorts/Reels/TikTok | `hokagechess.com/free-plan` or Discord |
| Tuesday | Fitness | Form check, workout clip, nutrition note, habit lesson, or coaching proof | Story poll or plan-request prompt | Free fitness plan / group opt-in |
| Wednesday | Chess | Community game, live-training clip, puzzle breakdown, or Welcome Wednesday prompt | Discord thread or short clip | Discord participation |
| Thursday | Fitness | Second weekly Legion artifact: recovery, consistency, transformation, or Q&A | DM prompt for warm leads | Free plan / coaching conversation |
| Friday | Chess | Boss Battle, weekend challenge, opening prep, blitz story, or Loot Drop | Discord Loot Drop | Chess lead magnet or Discord |
| Saturday | Bridge | Biweekly BR-1 publish; off weeks are bridge prep or micro-bridge clips | Cross-tagged short-form clip | Unified list/community CTA |
| Sunday | Quest Log | Newsletter recap by MP-7 track: chess, fitness, or bridge-inclusive | Community recap post | One CTA only: subscribe, join, or reply |

### Minimum viable week

When capacity is low, protect the rhythm with lightweight artifacts:

- Monday: one chess short or short-form lesson.
- Tuesday: one fitness short or form note.
- Thursday: second fitness short or Q&A answer.
- Friday: one chess recap, challenge, or clip.
- Saturday: bridge note, not full production.
- Sunday: Quest Log.

Wednesday can become a community-only chess touchpoint during constrained weeks.

### Full production week

When capacity allows:

- Monday: primary chess long-form or mid-form.
- Tuesday: fitness short.
- Wednesday: chess live-training or analysis short.
- Thursday: fitness short plus DM follow-up.
- Friday: chess narrative artifact or monthly Boss Battle.
- Saturday: BR-1 major piece on alternating weeks.
- Sunday: Quest Log plus metrics review.

## 4. Monthly Rhythm

The month repeats a four-week arc.

| Week | Chess emphasis | Fitness emphasis | Bridge / newsletter emphasis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Name the month's chess target: opening, rating band, tactic theme, or Boss. | Name the month's training target: lift, habit, program, or body-composition focus. | Quest Log sets the monthly thesis. Saturday is bridge prep or a micro-bridge. |
| Week 2 | Show the first measurable chess attempt. | Show the first measurable fitness attempt. | Publish major BR-1 bridge piece on Saturday. Quest Log tests audience response. |
| Week 3 | Go deeper: lesson, study breakdown, or mistake analysis. | Go deeper: form, recovery, meal structure, or coaching proof. | Saturday is bridge prep, audience question, or cutdown from Week 2. |
| Week 4 | Close the arc: Boss Battle, rating review, community game, or lesson learned. | Close the arc: progress check, client proof, challenge recap, or program adjustment. | Publish second major BR-1 piece. Quest Log becomes the monthly scorecard. |

For five-week months, Week 5 is a buffer week: no new pillar expansion, no new format launch, and no additional recurring commitment. Use it for recovery, backlog editing, analytics review, or audience interviews.

## 5. Time Budget Model (MP-10-dependent)

MP-10 must define Rob's real weekly content capacity before this cadence gets absolute hour commitments. Until then, use `H` as the weekly content-production budget after non-negotiable work, family, client delivery, and prospecting.

Baseline allocation:

| Bucket | Share of `H` | Why |
|---|---:|---|
| Chess / Hokage | 40% | Three weekday slots plus the strongest existing content asset. |
| Fitness / Legion | 30% | Two weekday slots plus direct revenue adjacency. |
| Bridge / Discipline | 15% | Saturday synthesis, biweekly major BR-1 pieces, cross-pillar proof gathering. |
| Quest Log / admin / metrics | 15% | Sunday recap, list growth, scheduling, analytics, and next-week slate. |

Example allocations:

| Weekly content budget | Chess | Fitness | Bridge | Quest/admin |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 10 hours | 4.0h | 3.0h | 1.5h | 1.5h |
| 15 hours | 6.0h | 4.5h | 2.25h | 2.25h |
| 20 hours | 8.0h | 6.0h | 3.0h | 3.0h |

Capacity rules:

- If `H < 8`, publish only minimum viable artifacts and keep BR-1 to biweekly micro-bridge format.
- If `8 <= H < 13`, keep all day ownership slots, but limit long-form to one primary artifact per week.
- If `13 <= H < 18`, run the baseline calendar with one major weekly artifact and one biweekly BR-1 artifact.
- If `H >= 18`, add extra cutdowns, live-training time, or deeper editing. Do not add a new recurring day.

Hard cap: a single bridge artifact should not consume more than 20% of that week's `H`. If it does, split it into a two-week bridge arc.

| Bridge micro-post / poll / clip | 30-60 minutes | Off-week Saturday |
| Quest Log | 60-120 minutes | Sunday |
| Metrics and slate reset | 30-60 minutes | Sunday or Monday morning |

Batching rule: record chess and fitness artifacts separately, then synthesize them for bridge. Do not try to produce the bridge thesis before the week's pillar artifacts exist.

## 7. Bridge Cadence

BR-1 defines the identity-fusion pillar. MP-2 defines where it lives in the calendar.

### 7.1 Biweekly major bridge piece

Publish a major bridge piece on the second and fourth Saturdays of each month:

- Week 2: principle bridge, e.g. "progressive overload on the board."
- Week 4: proof bridge, e.g. how that principle appeared in a real game and a real training week.

### 7.2 Off-week bridge lane

First and third Saturdays are lighter:

- audience poll,
- 60-second cross-pillar clip,
- outline screenshot,
- Discord prompt,
- newsletter teaser,
- behind-the-scenes production note.

### 7.3 Bridge acceptance rule

A real bridge artifact must include all three:

1. one specific chess example,
2. one specific fitness example,
3. one transferable discipline principle.

If any of the three is missing, it is not BR-1; it is just a personality update.

## 8. Failure Rules and Recovery

### Missed weekday slot

Do not backfill two artifacts the next day. Roll the missed idea to the next same-pillar slot.

Example: if Tuesday fitness is missed, do not publish fitness on Wednesday and displace chess. Move it to Thursday.

### Bridge not ready

Publish a micro-bridge or a Quest Log teaser instead of forcing a thin major bridge piece.

### Sunday missed

Send the Quest Log Monday morning as a "week reset." Do not skip the recap entirely; the newsletter is the continuity mechanism.

### Audience whiplash

If comments, unsubscribes, or retention indicate confusion, keep YouTube long-form separated and move cross-pillar experimentation to short-form, email, live, or Discord for four weeks before trying another long-form bridge.

## 9. Integration With Cohorts and Campaigns

Campaigns fill the MP-2 slots; they do not create a parallel cadence.

Examples:

- A cohort challenge uses Tuesday/Thursday fitness slots for transformation content.
- A challenge recap belongs in the Sunday Quest Log.
- A chess tournament arc uses Monday/Wednesday/Friday chess slots.
- A Hokage Bulk or dual-discipline campaign uses Saturday bridge and Sunday newsletter slots.

This protects Rob from stacking a campaign calendar on top of the standing weekly calendar.
