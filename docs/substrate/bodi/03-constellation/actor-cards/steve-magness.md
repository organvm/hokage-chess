# Actor Card: Steve Magness

**Domain:** Mindset
**Confidence:** 4/5 (Established Authority)

## Profile
Performance scientist, coach, and author of *Do Hard Things*. Known for dismantling the "macho" model of toughness in favor of an evidence-based redefinition.

## Key Contributions
- **Real Toughness:** Redefined toughness as making good decisions under discomfort and uncertainty, not enduring punishment.
- **Calm in Discomfort:** Showed that staying clear-headed under stress outperforms forced bravado and grit-for-its-own-sake.
- **Inner Drive Over Fear:** Argues sustainable performance comes from internal motivation rather than fear-based coaching.

## Relevance to BODI
Reframes the grind narrative so Rob builds durability through smart decision-making under pressure, not burnout. Counters the toxic "just push harder" model.
