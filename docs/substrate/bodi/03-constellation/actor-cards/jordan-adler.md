# Actor Card: Jordan Adler

**Domain:** Network Marketing
**Confidence:** 4/5 (Established Authority)

## Profile
Author of *Beach Money*. Known for building an 85,000-person organization by focusing on simplicity, consistency, and "making friends without an agenda."

## Key Contributions
- **Passive Income Definition:** Coined "Beach Money" as income that flows regardless of active work.
- **The 1-per-Month Rule:** Proved that signing up just one person per month consistently leads to massive scale via duplication.
- **Vending Machine Metaphor:** Views distributors as independent volume-generators rather than high-pressure salespeople.

## Relevance to BODI
Provides the philosophical backbone for the "Ambassador" layer (L4). Rob's desire for a self-sustaining system aligns with Adler's duplication model.
