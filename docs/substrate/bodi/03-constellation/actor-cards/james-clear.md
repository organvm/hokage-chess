# Actor Card: James Clear

**Domain:** Mindset
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
Author of *Atomic Habits*, the defining modern work on behavioral design. Known for translating habit science into practical systems that compound over time.

## Key Contributions
- **1% Better Compounding:** Popularized the idea that tiny daily improvements compound into outsized long-term results.
- **Four Laws of Behavior Change:** Codified make it obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying as the operating system for building habits.
- **Identity-Based Habits:** Reframed habit change as becoming a certain type of person rather than chasing a goal.

## Relevance to BODI
Gives Rob the design language for the consistency engine behind a creator practice. The identity-based and compounding framing supports turning sporadic effort into a durable system.
