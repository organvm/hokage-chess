# Actor Card: Eric Worre

**Domain:** Network Marketing
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
Founder of *Network Marketing Pro* and author of *Go Pro: 7 Steps to Becoming a Network Marketing Professional*. The most recognized voice in professionalizing the MLM space, treating distribution as a skilled craft rather than a side hustle.

## Key Contributions
- **"Go Pro" Methodology:** Reframed network marketing as a profession requiring deliberate skill development, not luck or personality.
- **The 7 Skills:** Codified a teachable sequence (finding prospects, inviting, presenting, follow-up, closing, getting them started, promoting events) that standardizes the entire sales cycle.
- **Event-Driven Growth:** Popularized large-scale training events as engines for belief, retention, and high-ticket conversion.

## Relevance to BODI
Supplies the playbook for converting an audience into a trained, duplicable sales force. Rob extracts the "skills are teachable, not innate" frame to systematize how casual viewers become committed coaching clients.
