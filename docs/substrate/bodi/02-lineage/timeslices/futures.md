# Futures

## Near-term lead potentials

1. **Passive attraction layer** - public reels become L0 and lower the share of weekly hours spent on cold scraping.
2. **Warm-first outreach** - recent engagers route ahead of fully cold prospects.
3. **Content-assisted retention** - plan delivery includes a curated content pack and live-QA invitation.
4. **VIP premium series** - gated deep-dives and case studies increase retention and ambassador readiness.
5. **Ambassador toolkit** - top content, scripts, and reading lists make replication cleaner.

## Future risk surfaces

- If the content layer stays ad hoc, it will increase work rather than replacing manual work.
- If ambassador onboarding is not codified, multiplier quality will vary too widely.
- If metrics stay informal, Rob will not know whether content is reducing manual hours.

## Best next research passes

- quantify the weekly time split across L0-L4
- inventory every existing reel by topic, platform, and engagement
- compare BODI's current CRM fields against the proposed content-pack fields
- expand the constellation from 5 named figures into categories of 25, then 50
