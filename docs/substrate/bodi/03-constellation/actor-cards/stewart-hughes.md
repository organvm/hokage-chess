# Actor Card: Stewart Hughes

**Domain:** Network Marketing
**Confidence:** 2/5 (Niche Industry Figure)

## Profile
A leader associated with Unicity, tied to metabolic-health products and the viral "Feel Great" protocol. Known for packaging a simple, repeatable daily wellness routine that spreads through word-of-mouth and social proof.

## Key Contributions
- **"Feel Great" Protocol:** Helped popularize a simple, named daily routine that gives prospects an easy, memorable on-ramp.
- **Metabolic-Health Positioning:** Anchored the offer in a tangible health outcome rather than abstract product features.
- **Protocol-as-Product:** Turned an adoptable behavior into the shareable unit, making the system itself the thing people recommend.

## Relevance to BODI
Shows how a named, simple protocol becomes self-propagating because it is easy to describe and repeat. Rob extracts the "make the method a memorable, shareable ritual" mechanism for his training programs.
