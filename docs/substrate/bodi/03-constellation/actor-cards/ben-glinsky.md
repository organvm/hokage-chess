# Actor Card: Ben Glinsky

**Domain:** Network Marketing
**Confidence:** 3/5 (Industry Authority)

## Profile
Founder associated with LiveGood, known for disrupting traditional MLM economics through a low-cost membership model. Positions transparent, wholesale-style pricing against the inflated product costs common to legacy network-marketing companies.

## Key Contributions
- **Low-Cost Membership Model:** Replaced high-priced product autoship with an affordable flat membership as the entry point.
- **Transparent Pricing:** Built a value proposition around near-wholesale costs, attacking the perceived markup of competitor MLMs.
- **Subscription-First Structure:** Reframed the buy-in as a recurring membership rather than a stockpiling product purchase, lowering the barrier to join.

## Relevance to BODI
Demonstrates how reducing the entry price and exposing the cost structure can widen the top of the funnel. Rob extracts the "low-friction, transparent entry tier" mechanism for the early membership layer of his coaching ladder.
