## 1. Decision Frame

The "Legion of Fitness" Facebook group is a legacy asset from the Beachbody (BODI) era. Its future has been evaluated against four options:
- **Rebrand:** Keep the audience on FB, shift the brand to "Hokage Legion."
- **Wind down:** Close it entirely.
- **Dual-track:** Keep it as a free top-of-funnel, run a paid community elsewhere.
- **Migrate:** Actively move members to an owned platform and then archive the FB group.

**Decision: Migrate and Wind Down.**  
The Facebook group will be actively migrated to the unified Skool/Discord community (as defined in FWS-2 and MP-1) and subsequently archived.

**Why:** 
Facebook as a platform contradicts the "Discipline/Overlap" brand identity (distracting, ad-heavy, low-accountability). Operating a dual-track strategy splits Rob's limited time budget (MP-10) and fractures the community. The goal is a single, unified "Hidden Leaf Legion" community ecosystem.

---

## 2. Migration Destination

Members will be directed to the unified community structure (FWS-2 / CWS-2):
- **Free Tier:** The Discord server (or Skool free tier) for general chat, accountability, and the "Quest Log."
- **Paid Tier ($49/mo):** The Skool premium gate ("Legionnaire" tier) for structured programming, cohort challenges, and direct coaching.

---

## 3. Migration Playbook (The 4-Week Wind Down)

This execution timeline integrates with the FWS-2 launch sequence.

### Phase 1: The Pilot (Weeks 1-2)
- **Action:** Run a small pilot cohort (e.g., "Legion Cut") using the FB group strictly as lead-gen. 
- **Incentive:** Pitch the most active FB group members "Founding Member" access to the new Skool community at a locked-in discount.
- **Tone:** Exclusive beta test, no mention of the FB group closing yet.

### Phase 2: The Announcement & Countdown (Week 3)
- **The Announcement:** Rob posts a comprehensive, pinned video in the FB group explaining *why* the group is moving.
  - *Talk Track:* "We are upgrading the dojo. Facebook is too distracting. We are moving to a purpose-built platform (Skool/Discord) with better courses, actual gamification (XP), and zero feed distractions."
- **The Countdown:** Announce a hard date (14 days out) when the FB group will be locked/archived.
- **The Funnel:** Provide two clear links: one to the Free Tier (Discord) and one to the Paid Tier (Skool launch discount).

### Phase 3: The Last Call (Week 4)
- **Action:** Daily reminders in the FB group. Highlight early wins and screenshots from the members who have already migrated to the new platform.
- **Urgency:** Final push for the launch discount on the Skool paid tier.

### Phase 4: Archival (T-Zero)
- **Action:** Do not delete the group. Change the group settings to **"Archived" / "Paused"** (no new posts or comments allowed).
- **Final Post:** A single, pinned post at the top containing the permanent links to the new Skool and Discord communities for any returning legacy members.
- **Admin:** Remove the FB group link from all existing Linktrees, YouTube descriptions, and automated email sequences.

---

## 4. Guardrails

- **Do not delete the group.** Archiving preserves the content history and allows members to find the forwarding link later. Deleting breaks the bridge.
- **Coordinate with MP-5 (BODI Exit).** Do not trigger this migration until the BODI exit messaging (MP-5) and Skool provisioning (FWS-2) are fully prepared.
- **Acknowledge the legacy.** Thank the members who built the community during the Beachbody era. Frame this as a necessary evolution, not an abandonment.
