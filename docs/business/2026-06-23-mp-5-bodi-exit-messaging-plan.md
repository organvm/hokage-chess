# MP-5: BODI Exit Messaging Plan

**Date:** 2026-06-23
**Foundation:** BODI Reformation Spec (`2026-04-25-bodi-reformation-spec.md`), Atomized TODO Inventory (`2026-04-26-rob-atomized-todos.md`)
**North Star:** Transition Rob away from the BODI distributor model without alienating his existing network or burning bridges.

---

## 1. Timing & Triggers

**When does the exit happen?**
The exit announcement must NOT happen in a vacuum. It must occur **post-first owned product** (Legion Bulk Protocol) and **post-community launch** (Skool/Discord). 

**Why?**
Rob needs a definitive "landing pad" for his audience. If he announces an exit before the new infrastructure is live, he risks losing his existing customer base to other distributors. 

**Execution Triggers:**
1. **FWS-3 (Owned Digital Product)** is complete, tested, and ready for sale.
2. **FWS-2 (Skool/Discord/Whop Paid Community Gate)** is provisioned and ready for onboarding.
3. The new coaching/fitness funnel (L1 to L4) is active on his own domain.

---

## 2. Sequencing (What Order to Tell Whom)

Communication must flow from the innermost circle to the outermost public audience to control the narrative and prevent leaks that could cause panic or resentment.

1. **Beachbody Corp / Direct Upline (e.g., Josh Spencer):** Inform them privately before any public or downline announcements.
2. **Current Downline / Referral Network:** Address the leaders and active ambassadors in his network next.
3. **Existing BODI Customers (VIPs & Active):** Personal outreach to his current paying clients.
4. **Public Audience (Social Media, Email List):** Broad announcement framing the evolution of his content and business.

---

## 3. Talk Tracks & Framing

### A. Beachbody Corp / Direct Upline
**Framing:** Evolution and personal brand growth, not abandonment. Absolute respect for the foundation BODI provided.
**Talk Track:**
> "Hey [Name], I'm reaching out to let you know that I'm going to be transitioning my focus away from actively building the BODI business. I'm pivoting entirely into building my own independent coaching and digital product ecosystem. I have immense respect for what BODI has provided me and the foundation it gave me over the years. I'm stepping back from active distribution to focus 100% on this new model and my own brand. I wanted you to hear it directly from me first out of respect."

### B. Current Downline / Referral Network (Level 4s)
**Framing:** Transparency and empowerment. They have a choice, and the leadership foundation he taught them remains valid.
**Talk Track:**
> "Team, I want to be transparent about the direction of my business. Over the next few months, I'm transitioning away from the BODI platform to launch my own independent fitness and coaching community. You all have been incredible, and I want to see you succeed, whether you stay with BODI or explore other paths. I will no longer be actively recruiting or building my BODI downline, but the systems and advice we've shared remain yours. I'll be sharing details on my new community soon, and you're welcome to follow the journey."

### C. Existing BODI Customers (Level 3s)
**Framing:** Upgraded value, continuity of care, and an invitation to the next level. 
**Talk Track:**
> "Hey [Client Name], I have some exciting news. I'm evolving how I do my coaching to provide even more value, personalization, and direct support. I am launching my own independent coaching platform and community! What this means for you: you can keep using BODI programs if you love them, but my direct coaching, custom plans, and community are moving to [New Platform]. I want you to come with me—here is a special link for my VIP clients to join the new community [free/discounted]."

### D. Public Audience (Levels 1 & 2)
**Framing:** The next chapter. Alignment with his core philosophies (Discipline transfers across domains).
**Talk Track:**
> "For years, I've shared my fitness journey and helped many of you get started. I've realized that the best way I can serve this community is by building something completely my own—a place where iron discipline meets life strategy. I'm stepping away from my previous fitness partnerships to launch my own platform, [Brand Name]. If you've enjoyed my content, my coaching, or my story, the next chapter is going to be incredible. Welcome to the new era."

---

## 4. Risks & Mitigations

- **Risk:** Downline feels betrayed or abandoned.
  - **Mitigation:** Communicate early, emphasize that their business is their own, and offer continued mentorship in the new community structure if they choose to transition.
- **Risk:** Loss of income before the new system is profitable.
  - **Mitigation:** Execute the 90-day phase-out plan (from the Reformation Spec). Do not cut the BODI cord entirely until the FWS-3 product is generating replacement revenue. Allow residual income to naturally sunset rather than burning the account immediately.
