# Strategy: Application Pipeline Go-To-Market
**Path:** `docs/business/2026-04-27-pipeline-go-to-market.md`
**Status:** DRAFT / CONCEPTUAL
**Core Premise:** Transform the internal "Application Pipeline" into a sellable product for high-end technical applicants and recruitment consultants.

## 1. The 3-Pillar Triptych
The product ecosystem is anchored by three independent but interconnected engines:
1.  **The Aerarium (The Bank):** Managing the financial incentives, referral fees, and "creative capital" earned through successful placements.
2.  **The Pipeline (The Factory):** The automated agent-driven intake, research, and application engine.
3.  **The Consultancy (The Human Layer):** High-touch advisory for applicants who want the "Inverted Interview" outcome without managing the agents themselves.

## 2. The "Inverted Interview" Viral Loop
Every application sent through the pipeline is a product demo.
*   **Mechanic:** Applications include a link to a "Self-Unraveling Narrative" (like the portfolio site) which showcases the system that generated the application itself.
*   **Result:** The recruiter/hiring manager becomes a potential buyer of the system, not just an employer of the applicant.
*   **Conversion:** "If our candidate is this efficient because of the tools they built, what could your entire engineering team do with those same tools?"

## 3. Monetization Model
*   **Q1 (Individual Applicants):** SaaS subscription for access to the Agent Fleet (Tiered by # of agents and deep-research depth).
*   **Q2 (Employers/Recruiters):** License to the "Deep Target Research" protocol to improve candidate matching and outreach sincerity.
*   **Q3 (Consultants):** White-label version of the Avalanche Handoff Protocol to scale their personal coaching businesses.

## 4. Phase 1 Launch: "Testament" Series
Launch via a series of LinkedIn "Testament" posts (codified in `ROB-ISOMORPHISMS.md`) documenting the build of the pipeline in public. This provides the social proof required to trigger the first wave of beta-users.
