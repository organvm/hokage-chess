## 1. Why rituals?

A Discord server without rituals is a chat log. A Discord server *with* rituals is a **community that breathes on a cadence** — members know when something happens, who shows up, and what the rhythm of belonging feels like.

Three rituals, one per phase of the week:

- **Mid-week catalyst (Welcome Wednesday)** — onboards new arrivals, prevents L2-leak
- **End-of-week reward (Loot Drop Friday)** — gives free value, keeps subscribers warm
- **Pre-week setup (Quest Log Sunday)** — sets intentions, builds Chunin/Jonin accountability

Combined, they create a **3-touch weekly rhythm** without overwhelming Rob's bandwidth (each ritual is ≤30 min of his time per week if scaled correctly).

This mirrors BODI's Teamzy warmth-cadence: regular, predictable, parasocial-but-scalable.

## 3. Ritual 1 — Welcome Wednesday

**Channel:** `#welcome` + `#general`
**Time:** Wednesday 7pm ET (sustained — same time every week)
**Duration:** 30 min Rob commitment (with active scaling, scales to 60 min as community grows)
**Purpose:** L2 retention. New leads who joined Discord in the past 7 days get a public welcome + a quick personal touch from Rob.

### 3.1 Script (template)

**1. The post (Rob, 7:00pm ET):**
```
📍 Welcome Wednesday — Week of [DATE]

Welcome to everyone who joined in the last 7 days! 🎉

This week's new villagers:
[list of @mentions — auto-generated by Discord welcome bot, Rob curates]

A few quick things:
1. Check #welcome if you haven't yet — has the lay of the land
2. This week's Jutsu drops in #weekly-jutsu (yesterday, in case you missed it)
3. Friday is Loot Drop — free training resources
4. Sunday is Quest Log — share your goals for next week

If you're new, drop a 👋 below + tell us:
  → your current ELO
  → your main format (rapid / blitz / classical)
  → what you want to work on this week

I'll respond to every reply this evening. Welcome to the Hokage village.
— Rob
```

**2. Rob's active engagement (7:00-7:30pm ET):**
- React to every reply with a relevant emoji
- Personal text reply to each new member ("Welcome [name] — saw you noted blitz; the Anti-Italian Counter-Slap [from Wk 2 Jutsu] is a strong weapon for your tempo")
- Mention previous-week jutsu/boss-battle naturally to reinforce canon

**3. Optional voice channel (8:00pm ET, scaled tier):**
Once Welcome Wednesday averages ≥10 new members per week, Rob hosts a 30-minute open voice channel ("Welcome Wednesday Hangout"). New members can drop in, ask questions, see Rob.

### 3.2 KPIs

| Metric | Target | Source |
|---|---|---|
| New members / week (Discord audit) | ≥10 by Month 3 | Discord |
| Welcome Wed reply rate (% of new members who reply to Rob's post) | ≥60% | manual count |
| L2→Genin Squad conversion rate (members who joined in past 7d → subbed within 14d) | ≥3% | Stripe + Kit tag |

### 3.3 Failure mode + correction

If reply rate < 30% for 2 consecutive weeks → Rob's post copy isn't compelling. Test: shorten the post, add a stronger-pull "drop a 👋" hook, or move to a more weeknight time slot.

## 4. Ritual 2 — Loot Drop Friday

**Channel:** `#loot-drop`
**Time:** Friday 4pm ET (consistent)
**Duration:** 15-20 min Rob commitment (can be pre-staged)
**Purpose:** L2 + L3 retention. A free, valuable resource — gives existing members a reason to stay engaged and brings lapsed members back to the server.

### 4.1 Script (template)

**1. The drop (Rob, 4:00pm ET):**
```
🪙 LOOT DROP — Friday [DATE]

This week's free training drop:

📦 [Resource type — see roster below]
[1-2 sentence description of what it is + how to use it]

[Lichess study link / PDF link / annotated PGN file / etc.]

This pairs with this week's Jutsu (#weekly-jutsu, [name]) — practice the position from the jutsu in this drill.

Drop a 🎯 if you're going to work through it this weekend.
```

**2. Rob's active engagement (4:00-4:20pm ET):**
- React to every 🎯 with a hype emoji

## 5. Ritual 3 — Quest Log Sunday

**Channel:** `#quest-log`
**Time:** Sunday 8pm ET (consistent)
**Duration:** 20-30 min Rob commitment
**Purpose:** L3 (Genin/Chunin) accountability + retention. Members publicly post next-week goals; Rob acknowledges; community holds itself accountable.

### 5.1 Script (template)

**1. The post (Rob, 8:00pm ET):**
```
📜 QUEST LOG — Week of [NEXT MON DATE]

Drop your quest for the week below. Format:

🥷 ELO: [current]
🎯 Quest: [one specific, measurable goal — "play 20 rated games", "drill 50 endgame puzzles", "study Wk 8 Jutsu position 3x"]
⚔️ Boss: [optional — what's the friction you're fighting? "tilting after losses", "time pressure", "depth in opening prep"]

I'll respond to every quest. We meet back here next Sunday for the post-mortem.

[For Genin/Chunin/Jonin: your tier-specific quest channel is also open]
```

**2. Rob's active engagement (8:00-8:30pm ET):**
- React to every quest with a relevant emoji
- Personal reply with a Rob-tailored micro-tip ("for the depth-in-opening-prep boss, try the [specific resource]")
- Reference previous-week quests where relevant ("last week you said tilt was the boss — how did this week's session feel?")

**3. Pinned weekly archive:**
Every Sunday's Quest Log post is **pinned for that week**. Following Sunday, the post is unpinned (replaced by the new week's). Old quests remain in `#quest-log` for retrospective.

### 5.2 KPIs

| Metric | Target | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Quests posted / week | ≥10 by Month 3 | Discord |
| Quest completion rate (members who post a quest AND post a follow-up next week) | ≥50% | manual count |
| Genin retention (Genin members who consistently quest = Genin retention rate boost) | ≥+15pp vs non-questers | Stripe + Discord cross-ref |

### 5.3 Failure mode + correction

If quest count < 5/wk for 2 consecutive weeks → ritual hasn't taken hold. Test: Rob privately DMs 3-5 active members asking for one-line quest, posts them as exemplars; the seed effect typically pulls participation up over 2-3 weeks.

## 6. The week, integrated

The three rituals form a **continuous weekly arc** that interlocks with Bridge Content cadence:

```
TUE 9am    Jutsu of the Week drops on YouTube (PRT-040)
WED 7pm    Welcome Wednesday — new members onboarded, jutsu surfaced in chat
FRI 4pm    Loot Drop — paired drill for the week's jutsu
FRI (last) Boss Battle drops on YouTube (monthly)
SUN 8pm    Quest Log — set next week's goal, jutsu becomes part of personal quest
MON-TUE    Members work their quest using Friday's loot + Tuesday's new jutsu
WED 7pm    Welcome Wednesday again — and so on
```

**Total Rob weekly ritual commitment:** ~75-90 minutes of active engagement (Wed 30 + Fri 20 + Sun 30 = 80). Combined with ~3 hours per Jutsu (PRT-040), Rob's Hokage-side weekly commitment is **~5 hours active, plus monthly Boss Battle (~6 hours one week per month)**. **Sustainable on top of BODI work** if BODI L1 manual hours decrease per the cross-pollination plan.

## 7. Tier-gated channel rituals (Genin / Chunin / Jonin)

Beyond the public rituals, each paid tier has its own gated ritual:

### 7.1 Genin Squad ($9/mo) — Sunday Group Puzzle
- **When:** Sunday 9pm ET (after Quest Log)
- **Where:** `#genin-squad`
- **What:** Rob posts one puzzle position, Genin members solve in thread, Rob reveals answer Monday morning
- **Why:** retention + community feel without 1:1 demands on Rob

### 7.2 Chunin Dojo ($29/mo) — Tuesday Live Session
- **When:** Tuesday 8pm ET (after Jutsu drops)
- **Where:** `#chunin-dojo` voice channel
- **What:** 60-min live group session — Rob + Chunin members work through a position-of-the-week together
- **Why:** higher-touch retention; social proof for Genin → Chunin upsell

### 7.3 Jonin Mentorship ($99/mo) — Bi-weekly 1:1
- **When:** by appointment, scheduled in `#jonin-mentorship`
- **Where:** private call (recorded for student)
- **What:** 45-min individual session
- **Why:** premium-tier value justification

### 7.4 Ambassador room — monthly facilitator sync
- **When:** first Saturday of month, 11am ET
- **Where:** `#ambassador-room` voice
- **What:** ambassadors share what's working in their Genin cohorts; Rob aligns them on next month's content priorities
- **Why:** ambassador retention + cross-cohort pattern surfacing

## 8. Onboarding bot config (technical)

When a new member joins:
1. Welcome bot DMs them: link to `#welcome`, link to `/free-plan` (if they didn't enter via lead magnet), link to next Welcome Wednesday post
2. Auto-react to their `#welcome` self-intro (if they post one within 24h) with 👋
3. Tag them in next Welcome Wednesday post (auto-generated list of past-7-days joiners)

When a Stripe purchase event fires:
1. Webhook → Kit tag (`hokage_genin` / `hokage_chunin` / `hokage_jonin`)
2. Kit webhook → Discord role bot → assigns role
3. Discord bot DMs the member: "Welcome to [tier] — your channel is now unlocked at #[channel]; next ritual is [time]"

**Tooling candidates:** Carl-bot or MEE6 for Welcome bot; native Discord webhooks for Stripe→role; Zapier or n8n for Kit-Discord glue.
