## 6. Timing gate

**Do not send before all three are true:**

1. **CWS-1 Twitch arc has run a visible traction curve.** The world map's whole thesis is that live streaming converts narrative-watchers into community. The pitch needs a Twitch row in §5 that shows movement — a sustained cadence (e.g. ≥6 weeks of the weekly Training Arc Tuesday) with a follower curve, not a day-one channel.
2. **CWS-3 Hokage Scroll has a non-trivial subscriber base** with a healthy open rate — proof the audience follows Rob off-platform.
3. **A recent content beat is feature-ready** as a sample (see §7).

If the Twitch arc hasn't started or has stalled, this atom is **blocked on CWS-1**, not actionable. Log that state rather than sending a weak pitch. The introduction is a one-shot resource — a flat trajectory packet spends it for nothing.
