# BODI Attractor

## The shape BODI is moving toward

BODI should become a business where:

- the first contact can be content instead of a cold DM
- personalization remains real rather than mass-manufactured
- VIP status feels like entry into a curated system, not just a purchase
- ambassadors inherit assets instead of improvising everything

## What BODI should attract

- prospects already adjacent to fitness, health, or self-improvement
- reusable content assets with clear topical tags
- examples from top earners whose mechanics can be transplanted
- tools that reduce manual follow-up drag without flattening the human relationship

## What BODI should resist

- generic motivational posting with no information value
- unmeasured busywork that increases content volume without reducing L1 time
- study filters based on demographic affinity instead of extractable mechanics
