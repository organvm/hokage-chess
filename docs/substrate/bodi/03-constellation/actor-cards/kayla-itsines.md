# Actor Card: Kayla Itsines

**Domain:** Fitness Coaching
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
Co-founder of the BBG (Bikini Body Guide) program and the SWEAT app, credited with building one of the largest female-focused fitness communities in the world from an Instagram following.

## Key Contributions
- **BBG Program System:** Packaged a structured, repeatable HIIT program into a sellable guide, productizing a workout routine.
- **Community-Driven Proof:** Weaponized user transformation photos and a peer community as the primary marketing engine.
- **App Subscription Scale:** Migrated the guide into the SWEAT subscription app, converting one-time buyers into recurring members.

## Relevance to BODI
Blueprint for the community + subscription layer. Rob extracts the playbook of turning shared transformation (rating gains) into social proof that fuels app retention.
