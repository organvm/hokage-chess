# BODI Attractor: The Ingestion Law

## The Core Philosophy
BODI is a **Buyer-Led Distribution Network** that operates on high-contact trust and "unique information" value. We transition from "hunting" to "farming" by building a passive attraction surface (L0) that feeds an active conversion engine (L1-L4).

## Ingestion Criteria: What Qualifies for Import?
Any external tactic, tool, or figure must meet at least **two** of these criteria to be absorbed:
1. **Reduces Manual Drag:** Automates or eliminates a repetitive task in L1/L2 without losing the "human-in-the-loop" quality.
2. **Increases Information Depth:** Adds a unique, authoritative perspective to the premium content layer (L0/L1).
3. **Enhances Retention Sovereignty:** Moves leads from rented social surfaces to owned databases (email/community).
4. **Simplifies Duplication:** Makes the ambassador workflow (L4) more turn-key and less dependent on founder improvisation.

## Rejection Rules: What is Deflected?
We explicitly reject:
- **Low-Signal Volume:** Tactics that increase "noise" (generic quotes, memes) without capturing intent.
- **High-Friction Complexity:** Systems that are too complex for an average ambassador to replicate (The "Jordan Adler Simplicity Test").
- **Cold Pressure Models:** Tactics based on "grinding" cold DMs without prior content-led warming.

## Adaptation Protocol: How We Transplant
When absorbing a successful mechanic from the **Constellation**:
1. **Strip Style:** Remove the aesthetic and persona of the source.
2. **Extract Logic:** Isolate the underlying conversion logic (e.g., "VSL to Call").
3. **Hybridize:** Inject the "Toxic-Free" or "Hokage" branding into the logic.
4. **Test & Measure:** Run for one week and compare against manual L1 hours saved.

## Expected Outcomes
- **L0:** Content does 80% of the warming.
- **L1:** Outreach is responding to signals, not scraping hashtags.
- **L2:** Personalized plans are data-augmented (wearables).
- **L3/L4:** Community and Ambassadors operate on "Owned Land."
