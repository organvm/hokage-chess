  critical path; compounds the value of every step on it as it

  drafts FITNESS_LEXICON; Lineage Cartographer drafts fitness
  apex-predator roster.

  DEFAULT_LEXICON, loses domain fidelity.

  unfalsifiable.

  gate.

  Cartographer and Hook Sharpener corrupts both.
