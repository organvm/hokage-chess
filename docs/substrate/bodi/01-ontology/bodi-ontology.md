# BODI Ontology

## Identity

- **Business:** BODI / Beachbody on Demand Interactive
- **Primary operator:** Rob Bonavoglia
- **Companion business:** Hokage Chess
- **Primary commercial pattern:** multi-level fitness funnel with VIP and ambassador layers

## Core entities

| Entity | Type | Role in system |
|---|---|---|
| Rob Bonavoglia | operator | Founder, content source, sales closer, coach |
| Cold lead | audience | Person already adjacent to fitness who does not yet know Rob |
| Free fitness group | audience container | First social container after initial DM response |
| Google Form plan intake | intake artifact | Collects email and personalization inputs |
| Teamzy | CRM | State machine from opt-in through conversion and follow-up |
| VIP group chat | paid container | Retention surface for paying customers |
| Ambassador | multiplier | Downline participant who sells products under Rob |
| Reel / short | content asset | Public attraction surface that can pre-warm leads |
| Premium content library | strategic asset | Curated collection of Rob's best educational and motivational content |
| Constellation profile | research asset | External figure studied for extractable mechanics |

## Funnel stages

| Stage | Meaning | Canonical actions |
|---|---|---|
| L0 | passive attraction | public reels, shorts, unique informational posts |
| L1 | cold lead acquisition | hashtag and follower scraping, DM opening |
| L2 | opt-in and plan delivery | free group, personalized plan, email capture |
| L3 | customer / VIP | product purchase, warm follow-up, private group chat |
| L4 | ambassador | recruit sellers from loyal VIP base |

## System relationships

- L0 reduces the time burden currently concentrated in L1.
- L2 is both a trust builder and an email acquisition step.
- Teamzy is the operational bridge from L2 onward.
- VIP retention quality shapes L4 ambassador quality.
- Hokage Chess is the nearest sibling system because it has the premium content depth BODI lacks.

## High-value claims already present in source material

- Rob's hardest and most time-intensive work is cold prospecting at L1.
- BODI already has a functioning funnel discipline from L1 through L4.
- The missing layer is a coherent premium content system that converts outreach from mostly cold to increasingly warm.
- Every post should deliver unique information rather than generic motivation.

## Active ontology tensions

- BODI is currently legible as a sales machine but under-described as a knowledge system.
- Rob's content exists, but its metadata, categories, and performance signals are not yet canonicalized.
- The same founder operates both BODI and Hokage, so cross-system transfer is structural rather than metaphorical.
