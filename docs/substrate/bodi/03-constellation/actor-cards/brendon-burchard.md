# Actor Card: Brendon Burchard

**Domain:** Mindset
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
High-performance coach and author of *High Performance Habits*. Built one of the largest personal-development brands by distilling sustained achievement into a teachable, repeatable set of habits.

## Key Contributions
- **The Six HP6 Habits:** Codified clarity, energy, necessity, productivity, influence, and courage as the measurable drivers of long-term high performance.
- **Clarity Chart:** Reframed "finding your passion" as a daily practice of defining who you want to be and how you want to engage.
- **Energy Management:** Positioned physical and mental energy as a deliberate input to be cultivated, not a fixed trait.

## Relevance to BODI
Supplies the habit-architecture spine for sustaining creator output over years. Rob extracts the principle that elite performance is a stack of trainable habits, not innate talent.
