# Avatar Archetype Canonical Spec
**The Hokage / Underdog / Dual-Discipline Persona**

This document serves as the single canonical specification for the persona Rob projects across all domains (Hokage Chess, BODI fitness, coaching, etc.). It crystallizes the "Rocky energy" implied across the BODI Reformation Spec, the Bridge Content pillar, and the world maps into a single, unified archetype.

## 1. Visual & Aesthetic
- **Naruto / Shōnen Aesthetic:** High-energy, visually dynamic, anime-inspired thematic elements (e.g., Hokage, Jutsu, Boss Battles).
- **Rocky Underdog Cues:** Gritty, sweat-equity visuals. Training montages, overcoming the odds, raw and unpolished rather than studio-perfect.

## 2. Voice & Tone
- **Direct & Unfiltered:** Cuts through the noise. Tells it exactly how it is.
- **Profanity-OK:** Uses strong language naturally for emphasis, passion, and authenticity—not just for shock value.
- **Level-Talk:** Speaks eye-to-eye with the audience. No guru positioning; he is in the trenches with them.

## 3. Story Shape
- **Parallel Hero's Journeys:**
  - **Chess:** The rating climb (from amateur to title-contender, defeating named "Bosses" along the way).
  - **Fitness:** The physical transformation / bulk arcs (breaking past PRs and plateaus).
- **Narrative Engine:** Both journeys are driven by the exact same underdog tenacity and systematic discipline.

## 4. What He Is
- **Community-Builder:** Fosters a tribe (Discord rituals, Wed Welcome, Fri Loot Drop).
- **Coach:** Guides others through their own transformations using proven, replicable systems.
- **Dual-Disciplinarian:** Operates at a high level in two seemingly disparate fields (Chess + Fitness) using a single underlying architecture.

## 5. What He Is NOT
- **Nutritionist:** Does not prescribe clinical dietary advice or pretend to be a registered dietitian.
- **Scientist:** Does not hide behind academic jargon or lab-coat authority.
- **MLM Evangelist:** Does not lead with recruiting or company-line rhetoric. The focus is on the *work* and the *results*.

## 6. Anti-Archetypes
- **Generic Motivational Poster:** No empty platitudes. Every piece of advice is anchored in action and systems.
- **BODI-Corp Shill:** Never sounds like a corporate brochure. He runs *his* system, using BODI as a tool, not an identity.
- **Hyper-Polished Influencer:** Rejecting the sterile, ring-lit, perfectly curated aesthetic in favor of authenticity, struggle, and real progress.
