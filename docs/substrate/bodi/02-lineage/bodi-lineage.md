# BODI Lineage

## Historical frame

Rob's current BODI system inherits from classic network-marketing and fitness-coaching patterns: niche prospecting, personalized free value, CRM-managed follow-up, customer conversion, and downline multiplication. The call transcript also shows Rob locating himself through mentors and exemplars rather than through abstract market categories.

## Present frame

As of 2026-04-25, the business already has a real operating funnel. The constraint is not lack of a sales process; the constraint is the amount of founder energy burned at the top of that process.

## Forward frame

The next meaningful step is not a new product. It is a better distribution architecture:

- public content becomes a lead warmer
- L2 gains retention assets
- VIP gets recurring premium material
- ambassadors inherit repeatable content and onboarding assets

## Relation to sibling systems

Hokage Chess is not just another project run by the same person. It is the nearest transfer partner because it already contains the premium content density that BODI lacks. BODI, in turn, has the cleaner funnel discipline that Hokage lacks.
