## Decision

Accept the core premise: Rob should be the product-facing avatar, not the BODI or Beachbody distributor.

The fitness side should be built around Rob's documented training arc, accountability, coaching style, and Legion/community. BODI can remain context, lineage, a tool, or a passive secondary affiliate layer where it genuinely helps, but it cannot be the hero, the first CTA, or the dependency that defines the offer.

## What This Accepts

- Rob-owned fitness and discipline positioning can proceed.
- The fitness reformation arc should treat Rob's journey, coaching, community, and accountability system as the product.
- FWS-1 through FWS-7, MP-5, MP-6, and BR-1 through BR-3 are unblocked under this framing.
- Existing BODI infrastructure can be mined for lessons, audience understanding, and continuity, but not treated as the center of gravity.

## Qualifications

- Transition, do not burn down. Existing BODI customers should be served cleanly, and the shift should be messaged as ownership and maturation rather than rejection.
- The avatar posture is not guru perfection. The product is visible reps: training logs, adjustments, accountability, community, and coaching from mapped territory.
- Rob can document what worked for him and coach process/accountability. Avoid medical claims, clinical nutrition claims, supplement-protocol claims, or credential-theater positioning.
- Do not overbuild infrastructure before proof. The first proof should be content plus capture: Hokage-style fitness training arcs, email capture, and a low-friction plan/coaching intake.

## Carry-Forward Line

Rob is the avatar; BODI is context/tool, not the product.
