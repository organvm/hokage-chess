**Date:** 2026-06-21

**Related Atoms:** FWS-4, MP-1

## 1. Decision: Separate vs. Unified Channel

Based on the Canonical Pillar Architecture Taxonomy defined in [MP-1] (`docs/business/2026-06-21-pillar-architecture-taxonomy.md`), the decision for the YouTube long-form channel is:
**SEPARATE (with Bridge cross-pollination)**.

*   **Channel 1:** Hokage Chess (100% Chess + Bridge content framed around mental performance).
*   **Channel 2:** Rob Bonavoglia Fitness (100% Fitness + Bridge content framed around physical programming).

**Why:** YouTube's algorithm relies heavily on CTR and AVD. Serving a fitness vlog to a chess subscriber will kill the video's metrics. We are setting up a separate channel for Rob Bonavoglia Fitness.

## 2. Format Spec: Weekly Long-Form

*   **Format:** Weekly Long-Form DITL (Day In The Life) / Training Breakdown.
*   **Vibe/Aesthetic:** Gritty, documenting the work, gym DITL, overcoming physical plateaus.
*   **Cadence:** 1 video per week.
*   **Structure:**
    *   **Hook (0:00 - 1:00):** Establish the theme of the day (e.g., progressive overload, hitting a wall, nutrition).
    *   **The Work (1:00 - 8:00):** Raw training footage with voiceover or talking-head style insights between sets.
    *   **The Bridge (8:00 - 12:00):** Connecting the day's physical lesson to a mental discipline or broader life strategy.
    *   **CTA (12:00 - 13:00):** Call to action for the Legion of Fitness community or 1:1 coaching.

## 3. First 3 Episodes Scripted

### Episode 1: The Foundation of Discipline (Why I Train)
**Title Idea:** Why I Started Lifting (And Why I'll Never Stop)
**Hook:** Rob is in the gym at 5 AM. "People ask me how I sit at a chess board for 4 hours without losing focus. The answer is in here."
**The Work:** Full breakdown of the "Legion of Fitness Beginner Bulk Protocol" push day. Focus on form, consistency, and the mental battle of the last two reps.
**The Bridge:** How physical endurance builds the mental stamina required for complex cognitive tasks.
**CTA:** Join the Legion of Fitness community for the exact push day routine.

### Episode 2: Breaking Plateaus (When the Gains Stop)
**Title Idea:** What To Do When You Stop Getting Stronger (My Exact Protocol)
**Hook:** "I've been stuck at this bench press weight for 3 weeks. Here is exactly how we break through it."
**The Work:** A detailed look at progressive overload, micro-loading, and tracking metrics. Training footage focuses on the struggle and the adjustments made.
**The Bridge:** Isomorphism between chess rating plateaus and physical plateaus. The importance of analyzing failure.
**CTA:** Apply for 1:1 TrueCoach programming to get past your sticking points.

### Episode 3: The Recovery Equation (Sleep, Nutrition, and Readiness)
**Title Idea:** If You Don't Do This, Your Workouts Don't Matter
**Hook:** Waking up, checking Whoop/Oura data. "You don't grow in the gym. You grow when you sleep."
**The Work:** A day of active recovery and meal prep. Discussing macros, hydration, and the discipline of *not* training when the body needs rest.
**The Bridge:** The necessity of mental rest days in chess. How burnout looks identical in both domains.
**CTA:** Subscribe to the Quest Log newsletter for a full breakdown of my weekly grocery list and macro split.
