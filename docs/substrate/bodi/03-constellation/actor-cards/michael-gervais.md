# Actor Card: Dr. Michael Gervais

**Domain:** Mindset
**Confidence:** 4/5 (Established Authority)

## Profile
High-performance psychologist and host of *Finding Mastery*, known for working with Olympians and elite athletes on the mental side of performing at the limit.

## Key Contributions
- **Reframing Pressure:** Teaches that pressure is a privilege and a signal, reframing high-stakes moments as opportunities rather than threats.
- **Mindset Training:** Treats the mind as trainable, applying the same rigor to mental skills that athletes apply to physical ones.
- **Presence Under Stress:** Emphasizes single-pointed focus and self-talk to stay composed in decisive moments.

## Relevance to BODI
Equips Rob with the elite-athlete model for handling competitive and public pressure. The "pressure is a privilege" reframe converts performance anxiety into fuel.
