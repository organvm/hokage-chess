# Actor Card: Shaun T

**Domain:** BODi Core
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
Creator of *Insanity* and *T25*, known for max-intensity interval programs and a high-energy, emotionally connective coaching style that built a fiercely loyal following.

## Key Contributions
- **Max Interval Training:** Popularized the inverted work-to-rest interval model as a signature, ownable training format.
- **Energy as Retention:** Showed that on-screen intensity and motivational presence drive completion rates and word-of-mouth.
- **Community-Driven Accountability:** Cultivated a participant identity ("Insanity" graduates) that converts customers into evangelists.

## Relevance to BODI
Supplies the community-and-energy mechanism: a charismatic coach who turns difficulty into belonging. Rob extracts how shared challenge and personality drive accountability and referral.
