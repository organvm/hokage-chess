# Actor Card: Tony Robbins

**Domain:** Mindset
**Confidence:** 5/5 (Globally Recognized)

## Profile
The most recognized peak-performance strategist in the world, known for large-scale interventions and a systematized approach to changing human behavior and emotional state on demand.

## Key Contributions
- **State Management:** Popularized the idea that physiology, focus, and language can shift emotional state instantly to unlock resourcefulness.
- **Resourcefulness Over Resources:** Codified the claim that the ultimate resource is the state you bring, not the assets you hold.
- **Strategy / Story / State:** Framed any breakthrough as a function of better strategy, a more empowering story, and a stronger state.

## Relevance to BODI
Provides the on-demand state-change mechanism Rob needs for performing under camera and competitive pressure. Teaches that capability is gated by state, which is controllable.
