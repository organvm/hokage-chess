# Actor Card: Greg O'Gallagher

**Domain:** Fitness Coaching
**Confidence:** 4/5 (Established Authority)

## Profile
Founder of Kinobody, an early and influential operator of the lifestyle-fitness digital funnel, building a brand around an aspirational "Hollywood physique" minimalist-training philosophy.

## Key Contributions
- **Intermittent-Fasting Lifestyle Funnel:** Packaged a distinctive IF + minimalist-training philosophy into a clear, ownable methodology.
- **Original Digital Funnel Model:** Among the first to run a full content-to-program online fitness funnel at scale, a template later widely copied.
- **Aspirational Identity Hook:** Sold an identity ("Greek god" aesthetic) rather than just a workout, sharpening conversion.

## Relevance to BODI
The archetypal lifestyle-funnel blueprint. Rob extracts the "ownable methodology + aspirational identity" structure as the master template for packaging chess improvement into a sellable system.
