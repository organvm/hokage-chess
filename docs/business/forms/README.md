## Form discipline

Every form has the same shape:

```
Header — what this is, who fills it, what it unblocks
Section A — minimal required fields (the ask in its tightest form)
Section B — optional context fields (only fill if the answer is non-obvious)
Section C — return instruction
Footer — links to the source artifact + any blocked-on items
```

Forms 02, 05, 07 are **single-decision** (one field returns the form). Forms 01, 04, 06 are **inventory** (repeated rows). Forms 03, 08 are **disclosure / production-prep**. Forms 09 and 10 are **internal compounds** that close the loop on the magnetic-figure thesis.

## Lowest-effort first to clear

`02 → 05 → 07` clears three Rob-side blockers in under 15 minutes total. After that, `06 → 03 → 04` clears the schema/inventory layer. `08` is the production payoff. `01` is the slowest (×70) but most parallelizable — Anthony can draft, Rob can validate.

## How forms compound

A filled form 04 (premium reels) plus a filled form 03 (Teamzy schema) plus form 05 (handles) is the entire BODI funnel L0+L2 specification — three asynchronous fills replace one 90-minute working session. That asymmetry is why forms exist: they let pre-work happen between sessions, so when Anthony returns the substrate is warm and the next session is pure synthesis instead of intake.
